Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our E-Health platform.

What is E-Health? +

E-Health is a digital healthcare platform that enables online consultations, appointment bookings, and secure medical record management.

How do I book an online consultation? +

To book an online consultation, sign in to your account, search for a doctor, select an available time slot, and confirm your appointment.

Is my health data secure? +

Yes, we use encrypted storage and comply with healthcare security standards to ensure the safety of your personal and medical data.

Can I receive electronic prescriptions? +

Yes, after your consultation, the doctor can issue an electronic prescription that you can download or send directly to a pharmacy.

Which devices support online consultations? +

You can access online consultations using smartphones, tablets, or desktops with a stable internet connection.

Are doctors on the platform certified? +

Yes, all doctors on our platform are verified professionals with the necessary certifications and licenses.

How do I make a payment for my consultation? +

You can pay securely through credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or other integrated payment gateways available on our platform.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? +

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment through the dashboard. Cancellation policies may vary by doctor.

Do I need to download any software for consultations? +

No, our platform is web-based, and consultations can be accessed directly from your browser without any additional software.

What should I do if I have a technical issue? +

If you experience technical issues, please contact our support team through the help center or email us at support@ehealth.com.
